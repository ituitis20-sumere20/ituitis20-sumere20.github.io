ABOUT THE APP

Key features of our application

Extensive EV Network

The integration of an extensive EV network within application, allowing users to easily locate and access electric vehicle charging stations.

Travel Planning

Facilitating efficient route planning and seamless travel experiences for EV owners.

Reservation

The reservation system feature in the PointCharge app allows EV owners to book charging stations in advance, ensuring access when needed.

Support & Service

Users can access immediate support through the app for any issues related to charging station functionality, app navigation, or their EV.
